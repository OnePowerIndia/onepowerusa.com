Green Hydrogen Technology

We are a green energy startup developing technology for harvesting Solar energy using the off-shore Green Hydrogen route.

Capabilities & Strengths

  • Developed by technologists having cumulatively several decades of industry experience in startups, high technology research, and engineering areas.
  • Our low cost high efficiency electrolyzers can operate on undiluted sea water, and represent a novel engineering solution to the problem of Green Hydrogen production.
  • Our proposed ocean device uses ocean currents and winds to both gather and transport the Green Hydrogen fuel from over large areas at negligible cost.

Applications

  • Shipping decarbonization and emission reduction through on-port and in-transit Hydrogen fueling.
  • Cleaning up ocean plastics via hydrogenation of micro-plastics within our speciality electrolyzer to produce fuels.
  • Improving the anoxic regions of the Ocean by releasing Oxygen deep underwater.

Timeline & Status

  • The company was founded in 2020. Initial efforts were aimed at in proving, developing, and protecting the technology through a series of prototypes and patent applications.
  • As a result of interest from multiple potential customers, the company is currently working towards manufacturing Hydrogen on-site for startups employing Hydrogen for energy storage as well as for larger industrial customers.
